How to Play the Drum Kit

To play the drum kit, press the corresponding keys on your keyboard or click on the buttons displayed on the screen:

  • A: Clap Sound
  • S: Hi-Hat Sound
  • D: Kick Sound
  • F: Open Hat Sound
  • G: Boom Sound
  • H: Ride Sound
  • J: Snare Sound
  • K: Tom Sound
  • L: Tink Sound

Enjoy playing the drum kit!
